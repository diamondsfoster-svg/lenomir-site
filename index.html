LENOMIR

Regulation • Reparenting • Rewiring

A nervous-system–friendly support app created for neurodivergent and sensitive minds.

Lenomir helps you gently track, regulate, and rewire the patterns in your everyday life. It’s designed for self-awareness, emotional support, and nervous system care — not for diagnosis or crisis intervention.

Trackers Restoration Journaling Vela AI Insights

Inside the app, you’ll find:

  • Mood, energy, and lifestyle trackers to notice patterns over time.
  • Journaling and reflections to process what you’re experiencing.
  • Restoration tools for grounding, bilateral focus, and self-compassion.
  • Optional AI reflections (“Vela”) to gently mirror patterns and next steps.

Lenomir is a wellness and self-support tool. It does not replace professional medical or mental health care, and it is not an emergency or crisis service.
