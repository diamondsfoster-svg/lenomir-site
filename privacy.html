Privacy Policy – Lenomir

Last updated: 27 November 2025

Lenomir is created by Lenomir Labs (“we”, “us”, “our”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the Lenomir mobile application and related services (collectively, the “App”).

By using Lenomir, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Who we are

Data Controller:
Lenomir Labs
Email: support@lenomirlabs.com

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, you can contact us at the email above.

2. What Lenomir is (and is not)

Lenomir is a wellness and self-support app. It provides tools for:

  • Daily trackers (mood, energy, lifestyle, body rhythm)
  • Journaling and reflections
  • Restoration tools (such as grounding, bilateral focus, and self-compassion practices)
  • Optional AI-based reflections and insights (“Vela” or similar features)

Lenomir does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or emergency services and is not a replacement for professional mental health or medical care.

3. Information we collect

We aim to collect only the information needed to provide and improve the App. The types of information we may collect include:

3.1 Information you provide directly

  • Tracker entries: daily check-ins such as mood, energy levels, lifestyle factors, body rhythm and period-related information (if you choose to use these features).
  • Journal entries and reflections: text that you write inside the App (for example, notes, reflections, prompts).
  • Restoration and module activity: which sessions or modules you open and complete (for example, bilateral focus or self-compassion exercises).
  • AI input: text you choose to send to AI features (for example, when requesting a reflection or insight).

3.2 Device and usage information

  • Basic device information (such as device model, operating system version, language settings).
  • App usage information (such as which screens you visit, feature usage, timestamps), primarily for performance, debugging, and improving the user experience.

We do not intentionally collect precise location data unless you explicitly provide it (for example, in a journal entry).

3.3 Account information (if added in the future)

If we later introduce optional accounts or syncing, we may collect:

  • Email address or username
  • Basic profile settings
  • Cloud backup or sync data

Any such changes will be updated in this Privacy Policy.

4. How your data is stored

Lenomir is designed to be offline-friendly and to keep most of your information on your device.

  • Many entries, such as trackers and journals, are stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms like app storage or encrypted preferences where supported.
  • If you uninstall the App, locally stored data will normally be removed from your device.

If we ever use secure cloud storage or remote servers to provide specific features (such as syncing or backups), we will explain this clearly in this Policy and inside the App.

5. How we use your information

We may use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App and its features (trackers, journals, restoration tools).
  • Offer you insights or trends over time (for example, showing patterns in your trackers).
  • Power optional AI features, such as Vela insights that reflect back gentle patterns or “next steps”.
  • Improve the App’s performance, stability, and user experience.
  • Respond to your support requests and feedback.
  • Maintain the security of the App and detect misuse or technical issues.

We do not use your personal wellness data to build advertising profiles, and we do not sell your personal data.

6. AI features and third-party processors

Some optional features in Lenomir use AI services to generate reflections or insights based on the information you provide.

When you use these features:

  • Certain text you select or type (for example, recent tracker summaries or a journal snippet) may be securely sent to a trusted third-party AI provider so the feature can work.
  • We aim to minimize what is sent—only what is necessary to produce the requested reflection or insight.
  • We do not sell your AI-related data or use it for independent advertising purposes.

Third-party AI providers process your data on our behalf in accordance with data protection laws and under data processing agreements where required.

You can choose not to use AI features at any time. In Settings, you may find options such as:

  • Turning AI reflections on or off
  • Controlling whether your past entries may be used to generate patterns or summaries
  • Resetting any locally stored AI insights

7. Legal bases for processing (for users in the EU/EEA/UK)

If you are located in the EU, EEA, or UK, we process your personal data under one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Consent: for certain optional features, such as AI reflections, notifications, or sensitive data you choose to enter (for example, period-related information).
  • Performance of a contract: to provide the App and its core functionality that you have requested.
  • Legitimate interests: to maintain and improve the App, protect security, prevent misuse, and understand aggregated usage patterns—balanced against your rights and expectations.
  • Compliance with legal obligations: where necessary to comply with applicable law or lawful requests.

You can withdraw your consent for certain processing at any time via Settings or by contacting us.

8. Data retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide the App and its features
  • Fulfil the purposes described in this Policy
  • Comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes, where applicable

In many cases, your data is stored only on your device and remains there until you delete it or reset the App.

Where we process data on servers (for example, logs or AI requests), we seek to keep it for the minimum period necessary for security, troubleshooting, and service improvement, after which it may be anonymized or deleted.

9. Sharing your information

We do not sell or rent your personal data.

We may share information only with:

  • Service providers / processors: such as cloud hosting, AI providers, analytics or crash reporting tools, who help us operate and improve the App. These parties are required to handle your data securely and only according to our instructions.
  • Legal or safety reasons: if required by law, or if we reasonably believe it is necessary to protect the rights, safety, or property of you, us, or others.

If Lenomir Labs is involved in a merger, acquisition, or similar transaction, we may transfer your data to the relevant successor entity, subject to applicable law and continued protection of your privacy.

10. International data transfers

Because we may use service providers located in other countries, your data may be processed in jurisdictions outside of your own, including countries that may have different data protection laws than your country.

Where required by law (for example under the GDPR), we will ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, such as standard contractual clauses or equivalent measures.

11. Your rights

Depending on your location and applicable law (for example, under the GDPR), you may have rights over your personal data, including:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • The right to delete your data (“right to be forgotten”)
  • The right to restrict or object to certain processing
  • The right to data portability (in some cases)
  • The right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

You can exercise many of these rights directly in the App (for example, by clearing data in Settings). You can also contact us at support@lenomirlabs.com to make a request. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

12. Children’s privacy

Lenomir is intended for adults and older teenagers who can consent to using wellness tools under the laws of their country. It is not directed to children under the age where parental consent is required (for example, under 16 in many EU countries).

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children who cannot lawfully consent on their own. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data without appropriate consent, please contact us so we can delete it.

If we later introduce a separate version of Lenomir specifically for children and caregivers, it will have its own tailored privacy terms.

13. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against loss, misuse, and unauthorized access. However, no app or online service can guarantee absolute security.

You can support your own privacy by:

  • Using device-level security (such as a passcode or biometric lock)
  • Not sharing your device with others without proper permissions
  • Keeping your operating system and the App up to date

14. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App, legal requirements, or privacy practices.

When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the “Last updated” date at the top
  • Where appropriate, notify you in the App (for example, via a notice or prompt)

Your continued use of the App after changes are published means you accept the updated Policy.
